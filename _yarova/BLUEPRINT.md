## The 4 Psychological Levers — Always Apply All 4

**Lever 1: Shame removal**

Say: "Your brain is not broken. The system is."

Why: buyer blames themselves. Remove the blame first. Trust spikes.

**Lever 2: Loneliness relief**

Show: a squad of 5 people, a Team Lead, daily calls together.

Why: job hunting alone is demoralizing. Community is the cure.

**Lever 3: Fear elimination**

State explicitly: we handle resume, interviews, background checks, until probation.

Why: the buyer's secret fear is "what if I fail week two of the new job?"

"Until probation cleared" removes that fear completely.

**Lever 4: Exclusivity**

State: 15 seats max. Simple assessment required to join.

Why: gatekeeping makes them want in more, not less.

## Copy Invariants — Never Break These

✅ One idea per line.

✅ Short sentences. Max 20 words.

✅ Plain English. No jargon without a definition beside it.

✅ Name their exact pain. Not "job seekers struggle." Say "you wake up to another rejection email."

✅ Binary only. "When X: Y. When not X: Z." Never "might" or "could."

✅ Brutally honest. No fake guarantees. Honesty = trust.

---

## Never Say These

❌ "Revolutionary" / "Secret" / "Guaranteed job"

❌ "Bootcamp" / "Course" / "Modules" / "Curriculum"

❌ "Typically" / "Usually" / "Might" / "Could"

❌ Walls of text longer than 3 lines without a break

❌ Fake urgency — scarcity must always be real
